About

Dr. Noha Hazzazi is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Howard University with a Ph.D. in Information Technology from George Mason University. Her research spans cybersecurity, secure communications, AI, and public interest technology. She is a dedicated educator and has served as an advisor and dissertation committee chair for numerous graduate students.

Research Interests

  • Cybersecurity in IoT and Autonomous Systems
  • Machine Learning and Trustworthy AI
  • Secure Mesh Networks and Satellite Communications
  • Software Reliability and Secure Systems Design
  • Public Interest Technology
